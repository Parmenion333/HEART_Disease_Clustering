Variable Overview
age = age of the person
sex = gender of the person (female = 0/ male = 1)
cp = chest pain (sclaed from 0 to 3)
trestbps = resting blood pressure (in mm Hg on admission to the hospital 120/80 max/min)
chol = serum cholestoral in mg/dl
fbs = fasting blood sugar & gt; 120 mg/dl (blutzucker nÃ¼chtern)
restecg = resting electrocardiographic results
thalach = maximum heart rate achieved
exang = exercise induced angina (Angina is heart condition, usually due to not enough blood flow to the heart)
oldpeak = ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest (ST depression = niedriger wert ist wenig blut zum )
slope = the slope of the peak exercise ST segment
ca = number of major vessels colored by flourosopy
thal = 1 => fixed defect; 2 => normal; 3 => reversable defect (Thallium is a marker and helps to indentify the blood flow in the heart)
target = heart disease (0 = no disease; 1 = disease)
